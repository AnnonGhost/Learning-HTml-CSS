Here Are Some Tips from My side.....

  1. Don't over burn yourself!
  2. Don't get anxious it's just a streak.
  3. Start as many as projects/orgs you can, collaborate with humans and contribute more!
  4. It took about 180 days for this to become a part of life.
  5. Make new mistakes, make them in public out and loud, that is one of the finest ways to learn.
  6. Accept negative criticism with a smile.
  7. Accept and work on all the positive criticism.
  8. Ask people about their thoughts on IRC, Social Media et.al
  9. Pause and reflect at times.
  10. Make a public list of TODO items, so that people will help you with it and you will be cautious about them.
  11. Make sure your projects have good readme!
  12. Make meta repos for ideating if needed.
